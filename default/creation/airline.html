Here, fill out all the pertinent information about your new airline.

  • Name may be what ever you desire, no special characters.
  • ICAO code must be three characters, unique from any other airline's.
  • IATA Code must be two letters, also unique.
  • Hub: Choose wisely, not all countries are open-markets. You cannot change this later.
  • Carrier scope: Matters for certain traffic rights, as well as for investors.
  • Carrier type: Matters for investors and the formulation of your business plan.

You will have more configuration options for the world, the CEO, and investments later.
